Dungeon Deathmatch

Game Description:

Dungeon deathmatch is a fast paced top down multiplayer deathmatch game. Fight up to 8 players per match in this fun action arcade game. Jump into your next deathmatch right away.

Controls:

WASD - Movement

Hold Space Bar - Weapon Wheel

Right mouse button - Leaderboards

Left mouse button - Shoot
