Paper Minecraft v11.7

Game Description:

Paper Minecraft maintains the core mechanics of the original game, like building structures, mining resources, crafting items, and surviving in a world populated with different types of mobs (animals, monsters, etc.). However, it's all presented in a 2D side-scrolling perspective rather than the 3D open world of the original Minecraft.

Controls:

PC: Mouse to look A & D to move W or Space to Jump E to interract
