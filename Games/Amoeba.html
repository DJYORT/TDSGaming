Game Page

Amoeba Online

Game Description:

Jump into the ultimate single cell battle! Compete against 100 players or bots in this multiplayer browser game. Eat as many cells as possible to help you grow larger then the competition! The larger you are, the more kills you will get. Although watch out for the hazard zones because if your in one when it turns red, you will go back to your initial size.

Controls:

PC: Follows the mouse

Mobile: Touch the screen to move.
